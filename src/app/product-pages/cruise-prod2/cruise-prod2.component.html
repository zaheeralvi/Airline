|Genting Cruise
| 5th April - 18th Nove' 2020

Day 1: Delhi – Shimla (350 Kms / 8.5 Hours)

On arrival at the Delhi Airport, meet our driver and then proceed to drive to Shimla. Have lunch at a nice Highway dhaba enroute. Reach Shimla by evening and check into your hotel. Evening is free for leisure activities, have dinner and stay overnight in the hotel.

Day 2: Shimla Sightseeing

After breakfast get ready for Kufri sightseeing. Kufri is a tiny hill station in Shimla district of Himachal Pradesh state in India. Visit Kufri Zoo, the highest point in the surrounding region. It hosts rare Antelopes, bears, etc. Later visit Mall Road Markets, Jhakoo temple etc Ideal time for shopping and eating out at restaurants. Have dinner and stay overnight in the hotel.

Day 3: Shimla – Kullu – Manali (250 Kms / 7.5 Hrs)

After an early breakfast, check out and start to go on a beautiful drive to Manali. Enroute try river rafting on the Beas River, do some shawl shopping at Kullu and then proceed towards Manali. Arrive by evening, check into your hotel and relax. Evening is for leisure, have dinner and stay overnight in the hotel.

Day 4: Manali Sightseeing

After your morning breakfast, proceed for a tour of Manali visiting the Hadimba Devi Temple - built in the tranquil and serene woods, Tibetan Monastery, old Manali and the club house. In the afternoon, visit Vashistha Temple to take a dip in the hot water springs and then have lunch at a local restaurant/café. Return back to your hotel by evening and stay overnight.

Day 5: Manali - Solang Valley - Manali

Enjoy your Morning breakfast at the hotel and later get ready for an excursion to Solang Valley, it is outstandingly beautiful and is an ideal spot to view the majestic snow-covered Himalayas, serene natural surroundings and to experience some adventurous activities such as Paragliding, Cable car ride, Skiing, Zorbing, etc. on direct payment basis. Kindly Note: National Green Tribunal is allowing only limited vehicle to Rohtang pass per day. So Rohtang Pass/ Snow Point Sightseeing can be done by own cost or by us if Permit is Available - Pay on the spot according to the Union rate.

Day 6: Manali – Delhi (550 Kms / 13 Hrs)

After breakfast, check out of the hotel and start to head back to Delhi.
