Day 01: Arrive at Kuala Lumpur

Arrive at Kuala Lumpur, Meet and greet with our Local representative and transfer to hotel. Check in Hotel at 2:00PM, evening proceed to Genting Highland with one way cable car tour Genting Highlands with a beautiful scenic drive along the mountains. Relax as you leave the busy city life and the hot weather behind you. Amid the fresh air, ride the fastest mono cable car in the world to reach the top of the hill. While on it, marvel at the beautiful panoramic view of the green tropical rainforest below you, return to Hotel, Overnight stay in Hotel.

Day 02: Kuala Lumpur to Bali

After Breakfast check out from the hotel, proceed to Kuala Lumpur city tour, and See the new King’s Palace, the National Monument, the beautiful Lake Gardens and the National Mosque. Visit National Museum, view the old Moorish style Railway Station and the Independence Square. At the city Centre, and then transfer to Bali, arrive Bali check in Hotel, evening proceed to Tanha Lot Sunset tour return to Hotel, Overnight Stay in Hotel.

Note: Supplement Charge for Petronas Twin Tower INR 2000/- per Person

Day 03: Kintamani and ubud Tour

Enhance your experience of Bali’s rich cultural tapestry on a full-day tour of the islands natural and cultural highlights. gain fascinating insight into Bali’s artistic heritage at Ubud, marvel at the dramatic volcanic landscape of Mount Batur, an active volcano down which molten lava spills, and immerse yourself in the colorful choreography of a Barong Dance, a traditional performance in which the struggle between good and evil is portrayed. Return to Hotel, Overnight stay in Hotel.

Day 04: Water Sports

Today free for Watersports activities on PVT transfer (1 round banana ride, 1 x parasailing, 1 x jet ski).

Day 05: Uluwatu Sunset Tour

Uluwatu Sunset Tour, Where the most iconic temple and spectacular view of the cliffs are the best in Bali. Uluwatu is our main highlight of this tour, which leads you to admire the beautiful Balinese temple nestled on the cliff top and overlooks the wonderful Indian Ocean view.

Day 06: Departure

After Breakfast check out from the Hotel, keep the luggage at Hotel lobby go for shopping evening drop at Airport for your onward journey.
